# Project Summary: Campaign Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Objective

Create a dashboard-ready campaign analytics project to monitor marketing performance across channels, campaigns, devices, and regions.

## Business Context

Marketing teams need to understand how budget translates into traffic, conversions, and revenue. A campaign performance dashboard helps stakeholders make faster decisions about optimization and budget allocation.

## Methodology

1. Generate realistic campaign-level daily performance data.
2. Calculate marketing KPIs such as CTR, CPC, CVR, CPA, ROAS, and AOV.
3. Aggregate performance by channel, campaign, day, device, and region.
4. Create dashboard-ready summary CSVs.
5. Generate visual reports for GitHub.
6. Provide business recommendations for marketing optimization.

## Business Value

- Improves performance marketing visibility
- Supports weekly trading reviews
- Helps budget reallocation
- Identifies high- and low-performing campaigns
- Supports executive reporting
